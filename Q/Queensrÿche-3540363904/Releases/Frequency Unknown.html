Trivia:
-Tracks 11-14 are listed as bonus material despite appearing on all editions of the record. They are re-recorded classic Queensrÿche songs.
-Following negative pre-release reviews concerning the production, the album was remixed and remastered by a variety of engineers. Both variants were sold.
-Despite being credited in the full lineup, Robert Sarzo and Kelly Gray only performed one guitar solo each.
